Caroline Sartorius - Age, Family, Bio

Older sister of social media phenomenon Jacob Sartorius. She enjoys traveling the world and she often posts Instagram pics from her travels on her account, which has grown to over 310,000 followers. She has been associated with the Alpha Chi Omega sorority at University of Virginia. 

Before Fame

She was a cross country runner at South Lakes High School in Reston, Virginia. She began attending Wake Forest University in 2015 and transferred to the University of Virginia in 2017. She graduated from Virginia in 2019. 

Trivia

She was a Daily Point of Light Award winner for her philanthropic work during a project in the Dominican Republic to help Haitian children stricken with poverty. She has posted many photos of her Australian shepherd, Bowie, on her Instagram page. 

Family Life

Her parents run the government consulting firm Social Impact.

Associated With

Her brother has dated Stranger Things star Millie Bobby Brown.
