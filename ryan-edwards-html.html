Ryan Edwards - Age, Family, Bio

Former reality television personality on MTV's Teen Mom for seasons 1-4 who rejoined the reality TV world with the revival of Teen Mom OG in 2015.

Before Fame

He gained national attention when he appeared on 16 and Pregnant in season 1, episode 1. He met the to-be 'teen mom' Maci Bookout when Maci was 15 years old. He was 20 years old when their son Bentley was born.

Trivia

He enjoys racing ATVs and riding motorcycles.

Family Life

He is an only child born to Lawrence and Jennifer Edwards. He and Maci ended their relationship in November of 2009. He married MacKenzie Standifer in 2017. He and MacKenzie have a son named Jagger and a daughter named Stella. 

Associated With

He dated Kiki Cooper, who went on to star on season three of MTV's Are You The One.
