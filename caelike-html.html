Caeli - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican YouTube sensation also known as Caelike who has accumulated over 15 million subscribers to her channel Caeli YT, where she primarily posts vlogs. In 2015, she was one of the presenters of Telemundo's talent show La Voz Kids and in 2017, she hosted the Kids' Choice Awards Mexico alongside Mario Bautista.

Before Fame

She studied foreign languages in college. She uploaded her first YouTube video, "Mi Primer Videoblog! HAHAHAHOHOHOHIHIHI," in October of 2010. Her channel was formerly called CaELiKe. 

Trivia

She appeared on the Nickelodeon series SKIMO as an extra. She's extremely popular on Instagram where she's amassed more than 9 million followers on her caeliyt account. In 2016, she won Favorite YouTuber at the Kids' Choice Awards México. In December of 2021, she posted a photo of herself posing in front of a red neon sign that she took on her trip to Cancun, Mexico. 

Family Life

Her full name is Patricia Caeli Santa Olalla López. She has an older brother known as MisaelVlog on social media. She dated fellow YouTuber Alex Wassabi in 2019. She also previously dated Wero, Alex Strecci and Yayo Gutiérrez. 

Associated With

She's done YouTube videos with music artists such as Paty Cantú and Jesse & Joy.
