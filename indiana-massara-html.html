Indiana Massara - Age, Family, Bio

Pop singer who released her hit single "Drama" in July 2017 on JJJ. She joined the cast of the popular web series Chicken Girls as Rooney in 2017. Her first on screen performance was in the 2015 short Crafty: Or (The Unexpected Virtue of the Girl in Charge of Snacks).

Before Fame

She started modelling after she got rejected by a few acting auditions due to her height. Her first runway casting was for LA Fashion Week when she was 13.

Trivia

She has 1.9 million followers on her indiana Instagram account. 

Family Life

Her full name is Indiana Marie Ella Massara. She has an older brother named Presley. Her parents are Ian and Julie.

Associated With

She appeared in a music video for Sean Cavaliere's boy band New District.
