Marcos Maidana - Age, Family, Bio

Argentine boxer who became a multiple-time WBA Super Lightweight Champion in June, 2009. He won his first 25 professional bouts.

Before Fame

His first fight in the United States came on June 27, 2009, which he won against Victor Ortiz via 6th round TKO.

Trivia

He was in a serious car accidnent in 2013 when he crashed into a pickup truck near his hometown of Margarita.

Family Life

His younger brother Fabian is also a fighter.

Associated With

He beat Demarcus Corley to retain his WBA Super Lightweight title on August 28, 2010.
