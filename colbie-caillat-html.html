Colbie Caillat - Age, Family, Bio

Grammy Award-nominated American pop singer, guitarist, and songwriter. She has collaborated with artists like Jason Mraz, including the duet "Lucky." In 2018, she formed the band Gone West. 

Before Fame

She gained interest in singing after watching Sister Act 2: Back in the Habit as a child. She started off singing covers like "Killing Me Softly."

Trivia

She had her first top ten song with "Bubbly" in 2007.

Family Life

As a child she was given the nickname Coco by her parents. She has one sister. In May of 2015, she and singer Justin Young became engaged; the couple ended their relationship five years later.

Associated With

She provided background singing and songwriting for Taylor Swift's album Fearless.
