Councillor Rejects Car Fundraising after Building Bridge with Funds Meant for Official Vehicle

- The Bayelsa councillor who built a bridge for her people with money meant for her official car has rejected a GoFundMe on her behalf

- Onem Miracle Tyna said the decision was necessary and does not mean to make anyone feel bad

- According to the 31-year-old politician, she was focused on making a lasting impact with the little she has at her disposal

ATTENTION: Don't miss trending Kenyan news. Follow TUKO.co.ke on Twitter!

Onem Miracle Tyna, a councillor in Bayesla state, has rejected a GoFundMe or any other fundraising campaign planned on her behalf by members of the public after building a bridge with money meant for her official car.

Taking to her Twitter page, the politician said she appreciates the kind gesture from the public but she would have to decline it.

Tyna said she is focused on making a lasting impact with the little she has at her disposal, adding that Governor Douye Diri of Bayelsa will support her to do more for her people.

"While I am focusing on making a lasting impact within the little at my disposal, I know and I believe without doubt that H.E Senator Douye Diri will support me two do more for the people. Let's all stay focused on being a true servant to the people."
