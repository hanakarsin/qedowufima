Alix Earle - Age, Family, Bio

Miami-based TikTok star who is best known for her makeup, fashion and lifestyle content. She most often posts about what she eats, what she wears, what she drinks, and where she goes. She usually goes to glam restaurants, bars, parties, and the beach. She's also been known to travel to destination locations like Turks and Caicos and Cancun. She has earned over 6 million fans to her alixearle account. 

Before Fame

She graduated from New Jersey's Red Bank Catholic High School in 2019. She was a marketing intern at her family business, Earle, where she created most of the social media posts. She's a member of the 2023 class at the University of Miami, majoring in business administration and marketing. She previously worked at Hazel Boutique in New Jersey.

Trivia

Her social media accounts began to take off in late 2022, when she was widely being labeled as the new "it" girl of TikTok. She had under 300,000 TikTok followers going into the fall of 2022, then saw her followers rise to over 2 million before the end of the year. During this rise, she was featured in publications such as E! News, Dexerto and OutKick.   She signed with UTA in March or 2023.

Family Life

Her family owns Earle, of Wall Township, New Jersey, an asphalt and construction company that specializes in building and paving roads. She has a sister named Ashtin Earle. She dated baseball player Tyler Wade in 2022 but the couple split after 3 months.  She began dating Braxton Berrios in 2023.

Associated With

In December 2022, she featured Selena Gomez in a short TikTok that utilized the popular audio clip, "What's better than one covergirl? Two covergirls!"  She appeared in a picture alongside Charli D'Amelio and Michael Rubin's 2023 Hamptons birthday party.
