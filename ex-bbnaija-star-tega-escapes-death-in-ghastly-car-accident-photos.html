Ex-BBNaija Star, Tega Escapes Death In Ghastly Car Accident

Former Big Brother Naija housemate, Tega Dominic has escaped death in a ghastly car accident.

Naija News reports that the reality star took to her Twitter account on Monday morning to share a testimony of the incident.

Posting photos of ‘her’ damaged car, the mother-of-one expressed her gratitude to God for saving her life from the incident.

The BBNaija season six participant asked her fans to help her give thanks to God for the safety of her life.

Tega tweeted, “Child of a praying mother. Thank you Abba Father, not another one tonight. All I am saying is, it is not yet my time. This new age and more is a must. Tell God thank you for me.” (sic)

In another news, veteran Nigerian singer Baba Fryo, born Friday Igwe has revealed how Davido refused to help him in his time of need.

In a video posted on his Instagram page, the veteran singer stated that when he was depressed his colleagues which include Davido and 2face refused to offer any form of support.

He said that it was quite hilarious if people like him who are popular was ignored then it would be ridiculous to believe orphanages with unknown individuals would be offered help.

Fryo went ahead to reveal how an NGO he is an ambassador to refused to offer any help while he was in need.
