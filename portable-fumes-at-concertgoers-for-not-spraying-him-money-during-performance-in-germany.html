Portable Fumes At Concertgoers For Not Spraying Him Money During Performance In Germany

Nigerian singer, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable, tackled concertgoers for not raining money on him during a concert in Germany.

Naija News reports that the singer took to his Instagram page to share some of the moments from his music tour in Germany and other countries.

In one of the videos, Potable, while performing, expressed displeasure at the concertgoers over the less financial motivation he was receiving.

The ‘zazu’ crooner at some point started ranting and asking the audience if they think it was easy for him to come to Germany to perform.

Portable who spoke in the Yoruba language urge them to start spraying money before he continued performing.

Portable Reacts As DJ Chicken Claims

Meanwhile, Portable has reacted to a claim by his erstwhile friend, DJ Chicken.

Niger News earlier reported that the Disc Jockey claimed that he had an affair with Portable’s wife and also impregnated her.

He said this during an Instagram live session over the weekend, while berating the singer for forgetting how their friendship started.

Reacting to DJ Chicken’s claims, Portable blamed his superstar lifestyle for the allegations, saying that the DJ was trying to use him to increase his popularity.

Speaking further, the Zazoo crooner urged his former friend to disclose which of his wives he had supposedly Impregnated.

He also asked him to disclose whether he is the father of his first, second or last child.

Portable added that he might be forced to order his boys to assault DJ Chicken once again like they did months ago.
