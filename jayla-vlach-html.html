Jayla Vlach - Age, Family, Bio

Oldest sibling of three featured on the popular YouTube family channel SmellyBellyTV. She is also known for her own self-titled YouTube channel It'sJustJayJay, where she posts additional fun family content for over 950,000 subscribers. 

Before Fame

Her parents created the SmellyBellyTV channel in January of 2015.  

Trivia

She and her sister Aydah were featured in the viral video "Jayla and Aydah Get a Haircut!!! | Epic Time at the Park | Family Vlog." 

Family Life

Her siblings are Aydah and Rory Vlach. In July 2020, she uploaded a YouTube video about delivering food to a boyfriend.

Associated With

She is the eldest daughter of Terra Vlach. Jesse Vlach is her stepfather.
