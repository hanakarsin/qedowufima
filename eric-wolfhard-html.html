Eric Wolfhard - Age, Family, Bio

Celebrity family member and pop culture figure who is recognized for being the father of Stranger Things and It actor Finn Wolfhard.

Before Fame

He graduated from the University of British Columbia before attending law school at Harvard University.

Trivia

He co-founded VLW Research, which specializes in aboriginal land issues. 

Family Life

Aside from Finn, he also has a son named Nick Wolfhard.

Associated With

His son was cast alongside Millie Bobby Brown on Stranger Things.
