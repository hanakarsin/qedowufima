Woman tells men to stop using their moms' names to give girlfriends false hope

- The Kenyan woman cautioned men against giving women false hopes in relationships by introducing their mothers' names in the picture

- According to Rawder Kidula, she was once duped by a man who asked her to send photos so that his mother could see her

- Followers agreed with her message with some women sharing similar experiences they went through while dating

Valentine's Day is around the corner and former radio host Rawder Kidula is cautioning men to stop using their moms' names to give women false hope.

While dating has become an extreme sport for many, there are men who rely on using their mother's name in order to make a woman stay with them.

However, the marriage show host asked men to stop making women feel like they are in a serious relationship by lying to them about being liked by their mothers.

''Dear men, If you are not serious with a girl, stop using your mother to give her invalid hopes. Like 'my mum said hi' or 'my mum cant wait to see you', it leaves us confused," she posted on Facebook.

She also cautioned ladies against being swayed by the 'mom-talk' vibe.

''Dear ladies, a mum is only a mum after marriage. Before marriage he is your boyfriend's mother. (Let us not argue)," she added.

The married YouTuber first started by sharing how one day, she used to date this guy who once asked her to take a picture so that he can send it to his mother.

She had to shower and do appropriate make up so as to showcases her 'wife material' side.
