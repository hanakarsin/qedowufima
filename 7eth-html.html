Zeth - Age, Family, Bio

Wholesome TikTok content creator and personality who is recognized for sharing a variety of videos featuring his young daughter. His popularity on the platform led to him amassing more than 4.7 million followers on his zeth TikTok account. He has earned over 1.3 million followers on his zeth Instagram account. 

Before Fame

He posted his first TikTok in November of 2019. In an early video, he went to the beach and used a face distortion filter effect.

Trivia

He created a dance duet video featuring Charli D'Amelio. In early 2021, he started a TikTok series called "Podcast With a Toddler" in which he and his daughter had comedic conversations into microphones. He also posts podcast episodes on his Zeth & Saylor YouTube channel. He dressed up as a pirate for Halloween in 2021.

Family Life

His daughter appeared in the majority of his videos. 

Associated With

In early 2020, he performed a lip sync to rapper Lil Uzi Vert's "You Better Move."
