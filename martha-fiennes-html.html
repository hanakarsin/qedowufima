Martha Fiennes - Age, Family, Bio

Award-winning English film director, writer, and producer who is known for her popular films Onegin in 1999 and Chromophobia in 2005. Chromophobia was the closing film of the 2005 Cannes Film Festival.

Before Fame

Her directorial debut was with the 1999 film Onegin which starred her brother Ralph.

Trivia

She won the Best Director Award at the Tokyo Film Festival for Onegin.

Family Life

She married George Tiffin and they have three children whose names are Titan, Hero, and Mercy.

Associated With

Her brother is actor Ralph Fiennes.
