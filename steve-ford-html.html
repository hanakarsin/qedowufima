Steve Ford - Age, Family, Bio

Reality television star who is known for his appearance on the HGTV home-improvement program Restored by the Fords. He specializes in contracting work and construction in Pittsburgh and shares photos from his television show as well as his work on modern homes through Instagram for his over 70,000 followers. 

Before Fame

His first restoration project was on his sister and fellow co-star Leanne Ford's home. She purchased a 1960's historic schoolhouse and reached out to her brother to help with the contracting work. 

Trivia

Prior to his work as a contractor, he pursued a career in skiing and snowboarding professionally. 

Family Life

He starred on the series Restored by the Fords with his sister and fellow interior designer Leanne Ford. 

Associated With

Other HGTV stars who are also known for their work in renovating old houses include Mina Starsiak Hawk and Genevieve Gorder.
