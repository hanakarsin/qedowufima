Maria Marin - Age, Family, Bio

Motivational author and speaker known for her best-selling books, columns, and radio show focused on empowering women. Her books include If I'm So Wonderful, How Come I'm Single? She has earned more than 1 million followers on her mariamarinmotivation Instagram account.

Before Fame

As a teenager, she struggled with her weight. At 15, she was diagnosed with juvenile diabetes. 

Trivia

In 2012, People en Espanol magazine named her as one of the 25 most powerful Latina women. She hosted a nationally syndicated radio show on Univision Radio Network. She was named the Most Influential Woman in Radio multiple times by Radio Ink Magazine. She served as an honorary national spokesperson for the American Diabetes Association.

Family Life

She dated businessman Richard Roer. Her mother died of breast cancer when she was nine years old. 

Associated With

She and Aliza Licht are both famous self-help authors.
