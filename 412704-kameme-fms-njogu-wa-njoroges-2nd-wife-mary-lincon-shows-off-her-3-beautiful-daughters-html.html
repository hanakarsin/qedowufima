Kameme FM's Njogu Wa Njoroge's 2nd Wife Mary Lincon Shows Off Her 3 Beautiful Daughters

- Njogu Wa Njoroge, Muthee Kiengei and Muigai wa Njoroge are among Kikuyu celebrities who married two wives

- Rumours of Njogu and Mary Lincon dating started in 2017

- Their traditional wedding was attended by top Kikuyu celebrities, among them musician Samidoh and comedian Muthee Kiengei

Veteran Gikuyu radio presenter Njogu Wa Njoroge is proudly polygamous. He is married to two women. His second wife is gospel singer Mary Lincon.

Njogu and Mary formalised their union in August 2020. Their traditional wedding was attended by top Kikuyu celebrities, among them musician Samidoh and comedian Muthee Kiengei.

Their wedding was the talk of the town, and they are happily married.

Below are five photos of Mary's beautiful daughters.

1. Both Njogu Wa Njoroge and Mary Lincon have children from their previous relationships. They all co-parenting. They are in blended families.
