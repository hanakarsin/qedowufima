Mirko Trovato - Age, Family, Bio

Actor best known for portraying Davide in the Italian incarnation of drama The Red Band Society. In 2018, he'd begin starring as Brando in the Italian Netflix series Baby. He is also known for his roles in the films Non c'è campo and Restiamo amici.

Before Fame

Long before the premiere of the Red Band Society he joined Twitter and posted his first tweet in December of 2013.

Trivia

Also a social media star, his Instagram has more than 660,000 followers. He has a pet pitbull. Some of his hobbies include music and soccer.

Family Life

He is from Pomezia, Italy and has an older sister.

Associated With

He starred in Netflix's Baby along with fellow Italian actors Lorenzo Zurzolo and Benedetta Porcaroli.
