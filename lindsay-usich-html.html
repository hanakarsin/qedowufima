Lindsay Usich - Age, Family, Bio

Photographer known for her relationship with Marilyn Manson. She's often done photography work for him and has had other work featured in Frank Magazine.

Before Fame

She was first linked to Manson as far back as 2010.

Trivia

There were reports in 2015 that she and Manson were no longer dating, but the couple was seen together later that year.

Family Life

She has a twin sister named Ashley who married James Iha. She and Marilyn Manson wed in 2020.

Associated With

Before dating Usich, Manson was engaged to actress Evan Rachel Wood.
