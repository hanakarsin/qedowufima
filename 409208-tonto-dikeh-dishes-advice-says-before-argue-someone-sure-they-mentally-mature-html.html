Tonto Dikeh Dishes out Advice, Says &quot;Before You Argue with Someone Be Sure They Are Mentally Mature&quot;

- Ex Nollywood actress Tonto Dikeh recently shared a word of advice with fans on Instagram

- The mum of one told his fans to be wary of the kind of people they converse or argue with

- The philanthropist revealed that she does not deal with anyone who would drop her IQ level

ATTENTION: Don't miss trending Kenyan news. Follow TUKO.co.ke on Twitter!

Former Nollywood actress turned philanthropist Tonto Dikeh has taken to social media with important advice for her fans.

The mum of one on her Instagram page said people should be sure that whoever they want to argue with was mentally mature enough to grasp the concept of a different perspective.

She continued by saying that whatever answer they get from asking themselves that question would determine if it makes any sense to argue.

In the caption, Tonto revealed that she has learned not to argue or converse with anyone that would drop her IQ level.

She said, looking back at herself as the queen of trouble; she was not sure it was worth it.

She finished off by mentioning how underrated peace of mind is.

The pretty lady wrote:
