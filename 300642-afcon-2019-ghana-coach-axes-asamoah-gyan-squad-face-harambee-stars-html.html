AFCON 2019: Ghana coach axes Asamoah Gyan from squad to face Harambee Stars

Ghana coach Kwesi Appiah has axed Asamoah Gyan from the Black Stars for the 2019 AFCON qualifier against Kenya at the Accra Sports Stadium.

Appiah has handed call ups to some debutants as he seeks to unearth some new talent with Ghana and Kenya already qualified for the tournament billed for Egypt in June 2019.

The squad which includes three players from Asante Kotoko will head to camp on Monday, March 18, ahead of the qualifier on Saturday, March 23.

The Black Stars will take the stage at 9pm and will be out for vengeance after Kenya beat them 1-0 in the first encounter at the Moi International Sports Centre in Kasarani on September 8, 2018.

Meanwhile, Kenya will be without Japan based striker Michael Olunga who was ruled out of the forthcoming group F match with a hamstring injury and was replaced by Zambia based Jesse Were.

Ghana Squad:

Goalkeepers: Richard Ofori (Martizburg, South Africa), Lawrence Ati (Sochaux, France), Felix Annan (Asante Kotoko, Ghana).
