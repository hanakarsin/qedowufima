Tinubu, Gbajabiamila, El-Rufai, Fayemi, Others Storm London [Photos]

The presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Ahmed Tinubu, on Sunday met with his presidential campaign and strategic team in London.

Naija News reports that the APC chieftains are are in London to support Tinubu ahead of his lecture at Chatham House scheduled for Monday.

The APC Presidential candidate will address the United Kingdom audience on security, economic and foreign policy.

Those in attendance at the strategic meeting include: Speaker of the House of Representatives, Femi Gbajabiamila; Governors Nasir El-Rufai of Kaduna and Abubakar Badaru of Jigawa; immediate past governor of Ekiti State, Dr. Kayode Fayemi; Deputy Director General of the Presidential Campaign Council, Hadiza Bala-Usman; Mr Wale Edun among others.
