Kemi Olunloyo Narrates Her Painful Prison Experience After Being Dragged By Tonto Dikeh

Journalist Kemi Olunloyo has reacted to the statement of popular Nigerian actress, Tonto Dikeh in respect to her time in prison.

Tonto Dikeh had asked Olunloyo if she wanted to go back to prison after the journalist asked the actress if she had anything to do with the demolition of her ex-lover, Kpokpogri.

The Journalist had alleged that Tonto is a drug abuser and would not pass a drug test if subjected to one.

The actress who was not pleased with the statement, claimed that she is aware that Kemi Olunloyo is mentally challenged and she might just be her cure.

Olunloyo in her reaction to the actress’s response stated that she is hurt by the statement which reminds her of her six months jail time.

Kemi Olunloyo who shared a video on her Instagram account wrote, “WHY IS TONTO DIKEH WISHING FOR ME TO GO BACK TO PRISON? This is so hurtful to me. Nobody can truly understand what I went through in Port Harcourt prison. I still battle #PTSD at 50% recovery till today.

“I only did a #Kemified wellness interview with Prince Kpokpogri asking him if her rejoicing had anything to do with the “powers from above” that demolished his house.

“I used this opportunity to ask her to join my #EnddrugabuseNG campaign and donate cash and or snacks to the campaign.”
