Zakaria Ghafouli - Age, Family, Bio

Moroccan world singer who is best recognized for having released the hit singles "Bahra Bahra" and "Hobino". He is also known for having appeared as a contestant on the singing competition series The Winner Is... on the Dubai TV network where he won first place. 

Before Fame

He started singing at the age of six. He released his first single "Al Ghali" in 2011. He gained further attention in 2013 when he appeared on the compilation album Rai n' Bled. He followed this appearance with another single titled "Lawah Lawah" which was released in 2014. 

Trivia

He is also known for having studied at ISCAE and went on to earn his master's degree in International Finance in Brussels. 

Family Life

His uncle was also a professional singer. 

Associated With

He shared a video of him performing on the Rachid Allali talk show through Instagram in May of 2019.
