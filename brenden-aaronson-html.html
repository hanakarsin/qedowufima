Brenden Aaronson - Age, Family, Bio

Professional midfielder that was first signed by the Philadelphia Union before being transferred to the Red Bull Salzburg FC in January 2021. He has also made appearances as a member of the United States national soccer team. He joined Leeds United in the summer of 2022. 

Before Fame

He began his youth soccer career playing for the Philadelphia Union Youth Soccer Academy in 2015. 

Trivia

He finished second place in the 2019 MLS Rookie of the year voting and held the honor of being the youngest player out of the five finalists to be selected. He has accumulated more than 200,000 followers on his baaronson7 Instagram page. 

Family Life

He is a native of Medford, New Jersey. His significant other Milana Dambra posted a picture of them on her Instagram page.

Associated With

He played alongside DeAndre Yedlin as a member of the United States' national soccer team in 2021.
