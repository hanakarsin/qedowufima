Leylah Fernandez - Age, Family, Bio

Winner of the singles title at the 2021 Monterrey Open. She is ranked at #66 for singles and #120 for doubles. Both rankings were achieved in 2021. Her leylahannietennis Instagram account, which has amassed 340,000 followers, showcases her competitions, games and training sessions. 

Before Fame

She competed in the girls singles final at the Australian Open in January of 2019. In June of 2019, she won the girls singles final and became the first Canadian girl to win a junior Grand Slam title. 

Trivia

She is fluent in English, French and Spanish. 

Family Life

Her father, Jorge, is a former soccer player of Ecuadorian descent. Her mother is Filipino. She has an older sister, Jodeci, who is a dentist. Her younger sister, Bianca, is also a tennis player. 

Associated With

She and fellow tennis star Francoise Abanda posed for a photo together on Instagram.
