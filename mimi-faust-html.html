Mimi Faust - Age, Family, Bio

Born Oluremi James, she is best known as a star on the VH1 reality series Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.

Before Fame

At the age of 13, she was abandoned by her mother, Olaiya Odufunke, after her mother became a Scientologist. 

Trivia

She owned her own estate and commercial cleaning service called Keep It Clean, Inc. in Atlanta.

Family Life

She was born in Newport News, Virginia. She has a daughter named Eva. Her father passed away in May of 2014. In 2020, she got engaged to WNBA player Tamera Young but the couple later spilt in May 2022. 

Associated With

She was in a relationship with Grammy Award winning musician and producer Stevie J.
