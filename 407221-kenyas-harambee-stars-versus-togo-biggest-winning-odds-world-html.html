Kenya's Harambee Stars Versus Togo, Biggest Winning Odds in the World

The 2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers comes to an end today, Monday, March 29, with Kenya versus Togo pencilled for 7pm.

Mozzart Bet offers the biggest odds in the world for a Kenyan victory on this match and many other top fixtures today.

Harambee Stars of Kenya play Togo in Lome tonight in Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) Group G dead rubber.

With both teams having been eliminated from the running of a place to the coveted continental showpiece slated for Cameroon, the fixture will merely serve to complete tournament formalities for nothing is at stake.

Harambee Stars may have come short in their bid for a seventh appearance in AFCON finals but their gutsy performance against a star-studded African powerhouse that is Egypt puts Kenya in good stead to upset their hosts.

Togo picked a point in Nairobi during the last meeting between the two in November 2019, just days after Harambee Stars technical bench was completely overhauled.

The Kenyan team looks in better shape since the last time they faced Togo and even though are not favoured to carry the day on away grounds, the prospect of an upset is very high.

Nine places separate Eerste Divisie sides Dordrecht and Telstar with the later ranked in the top half of the table while the former are second from bottom.

Telstar but behind their three game losing run with a resounding 3-1 thumping of Roda in their last Eerste Divisie outing and look to build on the momentum to trounce their hosts Dordrecht to hit the forty-point mark.
