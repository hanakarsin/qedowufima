What happened to Michael Richards from Seinfeld?

Michael Richards is an American actor and a stand-up comedian. He is remembered for playing Cosmos Kramer in Seinfeld, which was a hit sitcom television series. The sitcom aired on NBC for close to a decade, where he was an integral part of its cast. At some point, he made racially insensitive remarks that saw his career face a series of setbacks. So, what happened to Michael Richards?

Undoubtedly, the showbiz scene has its own challenges, and fame is one of them. Failure to manage fame adequately has seen many successful celebrities lose their worthy careers. For instance, a "slip of the tongue" by Michael Richards has been haunting him for years.

1998: The end of Seinfeld

Richards enjoyed a successful career starring as Kramer on Seinfeld. The actor was a darling for many movie lovers up to 1998. He did not only become a star on the show but also won several awards due to his role.

Interestingly, he bagged more Emmys than any other actor who starred on the sitcom by the time it ended in 1998 – he is a three-time winner of the Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series (1993, 1994, and 1997).

2000: The Michael Richards Show

In 2000, he created The Michael Richards Show alongside other creative people. Unfortunately, the show was cancelled a few months after its premiere. The show received poor ratings, forcing the network to cancel it.

Another factor that led to the cancellation of the show was competition. During the time, his show was competing with JAG, Who Wants to Be a Millionaire, and That ‘70s Show. They had better audience ratings as compared to his show.

2006: The racist rant

Michael started to concentrate on stand-up comedy. He made regular appearances on Laugh Factory – a chain of comedy club established by Jaime Masada. However, in 2006, he was involved in an incident that has been haunting him for years.

What exactly is known about the Michael Richards scandal? The incident happened when the comedian was performing to a Laugh Factory audience in California.

While on stage, a black audience accompanied with a few Mexicans walked in while the comedian was performing. Before settling down, they started to order drinks. In the process, they caused a little bit of noise and disturbance, forcing him to interrupt his monologue.

He started to yell at them in an attempt to restore calm in the room. However, the incident escalated when he used an N-word on the black man. He continuously shouted the offensive word, angering the black audience.
