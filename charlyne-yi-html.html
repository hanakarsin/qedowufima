Charlyne Yi - Age, Family, Bio

Comedienne and actress who became known for her starring role in the film Paper Heart, which she produced. Her other film credits include Cloverfield, Semi-Pro, and This is 40. In 2015, she began providing the voice for the character Chloe Park on the Cartoon Network series We Bare Bears.

Before Fame

She made her debut on the hit film Knocked Up as a member of the perpetually stoned group. Before getting into acting, she attended the University of California, Riverside, but left school to pursue a career in comedy. 

Trivia

She was chosen in 2009 as one of Venus Zine's 25 under 25 women. She has appeared in the show House M.D., as well as the cartoon Steven Universe.

Family Life

Her parents are Luciano and Lydia Yi. 

Associated With

She joined the cast of House with Hugh Laurie.
