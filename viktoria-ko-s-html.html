Viktoria Kay - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram and fitness model who is known for posting fashion, activewear, and lifestyle photos to her viktoria_kay account. She went on to amass over 900,000 followers on the platform and millions of views on YouTube.

Before Fame

She moved to Los Angeles at the age of 21 and faced many hardships as a foreigner. She later obtained a degree from California State University, Long Beach. She has always been very athletic, playing tennis all her life including college.

Trivia

She modeled for Forgiato Wheels at the 2016 Los Angeles Auto Show and Dubshow Tour.

Family Life

Her full name is Viktoria Koos. She was born in Budapest, Hungary and grew up in Germany.

Associated With

She was photographed with rapper Master P in early 2016.
