Amidst Being Dragged By Nigerians, Yul Edochie Angrily Makes Demand For Deborah Samuel

Veteran Nollywood actor Yul Edochie has condemned the burning of a female student, Miss Deborah Samuel, of the Shehu Shagari College of Education in Sokoto State.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Deborah was lynched and burnt alive on Thursday by some Muslim colleagues who accused her of blaspheming Prophet Muhammad.

The students later filmed the incident and proceeded to post it on social media.

Reacting to the barbaric actions of the students, Yul Edochie, called for the immediate probe of the gruesome killings.

He explained that God is powerful enough to fight his battles by himself.

He wrote on Twitter: “I condemn the killing of Deborah. The perpetrators MUST be brought to justice.

“God who created all humans and the earth is powerful enough to fight his battles.

“Yes I want to be President, whether you vote for me or not, my ambition will never stop me from speaking the truth.”
