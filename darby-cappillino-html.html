Darby Cappillino - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and singer who was featured in the music video of MattyB's 2016 single "You." She has also been featured on the YouTube channel Dae3.

Before Fame

She previously did various voice over, video and vocal work, in addition to local theater.

Trivia

She was a cast member of the acclaimed preschool series The Mother Goose Playhouse in 2014-2015.

Family Life

She was born Darby Mae Cappillino in Middle Tennessee.

Associated With

She and Duck Dynasty star Sadie Robertson were cast in a 2016 film called I'm Not Ashamed.
