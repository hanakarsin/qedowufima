Sarah Pidgeon - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who rose to widespread acclaim for starring as Leah Rilke on the Amazon Prime original series The Wilds. She earned critical acclaim during her debut in the series' first season in 2020. It was announced in early 2021 that the show would return for a second season. 

Before Fame

She earned a BFA from Carnegie Mellon University School of Drama in 2018. 

Trivia

She guest starred as Jane Cartwright in a 2019 episode of Gotham. She also guest starred in a 2018 episode of One Dollar. 

Family Life

She has an older sister named Julia. Her parents' names are Jim and Julie.

Associated With

She stars alongside Mia Healey on the Amazon original The Wilds.
