Jana Maradona - Age, Family, Bio

Social media starlet who is famous for her self-titled Instagram account. She has earned massive popularity there for her fashion, beauty, and lifestyle modeling alongside glamorous lifestyle blogging. 

Before Fame

She began her Instagram account in May 2014. 

Trivia

She has seen her Instagram account grow to boast more than 310,000 followers. She has been profiled in La Nacion and Ciudad Magazine. 

Family Life

Her father is Argentinian soccer legend Diego Maradona. She has four half-siblings named Diego Fernando, Diego Armando Jr., Dalma and Gianinna. 

Associated With

She met her father Diego Maradona for the first time in 2014.
