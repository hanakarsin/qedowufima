Lil Phat - Trivia, Family, Bio

Southern rap artist who was tragically murdered after being shot six times. He notably appeared on albums by Webbie and Lil Boosie.

Before Fame

He signed his first contract with Trill Entertainment at age 15.

Trivia

His first studio album, Y.N.I.C., was posthumously released in 2013.

Family Life

He was at a hospital waiting for his daughter to be born when he was shot and killed.

Associated With

He collaborated with Webbie on the 2010 mixtape Trill 4 Life.
