Soludo Declared Winner In Seven LGAs Already Announced By INEC

The candidate of the All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA), Professor Charles Soludo is in a leading position based on results from seven local government areas in the Anambra State governorship election.

Naija News reports that the former CBN governor leads other candidates in Saturday’s governorship election in Anambra State with seven out of twenty-one local government area results announced.

The local government areas where Soludo has earned victory are Onitsha South, Orumba South, Anaocha, Anambra East, Akwa South, Njikoka, and Aguata.

Meanwhile, Naija News earlier reported that the Governorship candidate of the All Progressives Grand Alliance, Charles Soludo has secured the highest votes in Aguata Local Government Area.

Valentine Ozigbo, the candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Andy Uba of the All Progressives Congress and Soludo are all from Aguata.

The Collation Officer for Aguata, Prof. Anim Ajake while announcing results at the Local Government Collation Centre, said Soludo polled 9,136 votes, while Uba had 4,773 votes and Ozigbo trailed with 3,798.

Ajake disclosed that elections did not hold in certain areas.

He listed these areas as Umuchu 1 (3 Units – 2292), Igbokwu 1 (3-926) 5units 2665, Ekwulobia II (2 units).
