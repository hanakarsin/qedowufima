Graser - Age, Family, Bio

Canadian Minecraft YouTuber who was the organizer of Cube SMP server. His personal YouTube channel has more than 2.9 million subscribers. His "Minecraft UHC but you can craft items from any block.." video has received over 10 million views since it was published in February of 2020. 

Before Fame

He started his channel by posting gaming videos of Club Penguin in 2007. He also used to post Poptropica walkthroughs.

Trivia

A photo of what he actually looks like first surfaced in August of 2014 while he was in attendance at PAX Prime. The photo was posted by HBomb94 on Twitter. Since then, he has posted multiple pictures of himself on his Graser Instagram account. 

Family Life

His real name is Zachary. He has two brothers named Josh and Adam. He has three cats.

Associated With

Gamers who joined his Cube SMP server include Kiingtong, MineplexOfficial, and HBomb94.
