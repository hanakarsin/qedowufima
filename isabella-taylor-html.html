Isabella Rose Taylor - Age, Family, Bio

Fashion designer and painter, she got her own junior fashion line at Nordstrom when she was just 13 years old. She has her own YouTube channel, where she vlogs about her experiences. She launched her own Nordstrom clothing line in 2013.

Before Fame

She attended a sewing camp when she was 8 years old and began making her own clothes soon after. When her friends began asking her to make clothes for them, it didn't take long before she had her own truck show at an Austin clothing boutique. 

Trivia

When she was 9 years old, her parents got in touch with a coach at Fashion Accelerator 360, a New York-based fashion-business mentorship program.

Family Life

Her parents are both in the field of medicine and had no idea how to help their daughter become a fashion designer. It was their support that brought her to her agent.

Associated With

She was mentored and introduced to the fashion business by the same Fashion Accelerator 360 agent as first season winner of Project Runway, Jay McCaroll.
