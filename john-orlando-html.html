Johnny Orlando - Age, Family, Bio

Gained a following by posting covers of pop songs by artists such as Austin Mahone and Justin Bieber to his YouTube channel, oftentimes alongside his older sister Darian. He is known for such original songs as "What If" and "Missing You." In 2018, he was cast to star in Total Eclipse. 

Before Fame

He started making videos when he was 8 years old and Darian was 13. He and Darian began by creating videos for original songs titled "Summertime," "Replay," and "Never Give Up." 

Trivia

He has over 4.7 million subscribers to his YouTube channel, as well as over 10 million followers to his TikTok account. He appeared on the cover of the March/April 2018 issue of TigerBeat magazine. 

Family Life

He was born in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada to Meredith and Dale Orlando. He has two older sisters, Darian and Madison, and a younger sister, Lauren. He dated Mia Terrence from October 2021 to October 2022. 

Associated With

He collaborated with Mackenzie Ziegler on the 2016 song "Day and Night."
