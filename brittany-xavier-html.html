Brittany Xavier - Age, Family, Bio

Fashion, lifestyle and travel blogger who was awarded the Breakthrough Fashion Blogger Of The Year honor by the 2015 Bloglovin' Awards. Her blog is titled Thrifts and Threads.

Before Fame

She created her acclaimed blog in December of 2013.

Trivia

She is an Instagram phenomenon with more than 1.6 million followers.

Family Life

Her husband's name is Anthony, and she has a daughter named Jadyn. Anthony is a street-style photographer. On August 16, 2021, the couple welcomed a daughter named Poppy. She has two sisters, Bailey Molina and Nattali Molina. 

Associated With

She and Camila Coelho were both honored with Bloglovin' Award honors in 2015.
