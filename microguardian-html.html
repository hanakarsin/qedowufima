MicroGuardian - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator on YouTube who publishes family-friendly gaming videos from Minecraft and Roblox. His channel has garnered more than 700,000 subscribers. He has also posted a variety of vlogs, Fortnite videos and Pokemon videos. 

Before Fame

He launched his channel in May of 2013, uploading a first video just over two years later called "Welcome to the Channel! Minecraft and More!" 

Trivia

Some of his most watched videos feature fellow YouTube gamers SallyGreenGamer and Gamer Chad. He received over 24 million views on a Roblox video he posted in July of 2017. 

Family Life

His real name is Ryan Michael. He shared a picture with his brother to his Instagram account in May 2019. He has been in a relationship with social media star Dollastic, whom he proposed to in April of 2018. They married in January 2019.

Associated With

Along with Sally and Chad, he has collaborated with ExoRandy on his channel.
