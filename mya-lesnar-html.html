Mya Lynn Lesnar - Age, Family, Bio

Most well known as the daughter of former WWE King of the Ring Brock Lesnar. 

Before Fame

Her parents were engaged, however her father broke off the engagement in favor of his relationship with fellow wrestler Rena Mero. 

Trivia

Her mother has been a fitness model and has written for Deer & Deer Hunting Magazine. 

Family Life

Her mother's name is Nicole McClain. She has a twin brother named Luke. 

Associated With

Her stepmother is wrestler Sable. Through her, she has two half-brothers, Duke and Turk Lesnar.
