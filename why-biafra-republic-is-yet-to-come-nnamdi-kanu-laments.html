Why Biafra Republic Is Yet To Come

The leader of the Indigenous People of Biafra (IPOB), Nnamdi Kanu, says “enemies who are within are using the name of Biafra to deceive” which is why the Biafra Republic is delayed.

Naija News reports the IPOB leader, who is on exile, made this claim on Wednesday, June 3, during a live broadcast on Radio Biafra.

The IPOB leader, who is also the Director of Radio Biafra, lamented that the enemies within, who claim to be agitators are using the name of Biafra, to miss direct the attention of those that believe in the agitation for the Republic of Biafra, adding that they are “doing all of these things in order to delay the coming of Biafra.”

Kanu lamented that the Biafra agitators whom he described as “enemies within“, said they  all doing all this to “please their Janjaweed masters in the North” and that he (Kanu) and those actually fighting for Biafra, have “discovered their tricks, we know what they are up to, and we are determined to stop them, and that is what we are going to do every blessed day that we wake up.”

The IPOB leader said on Radio  Biafra: “The only thing is that our enemies who are within are using the name of Biafra, they are telling you that they are agitator to deceive, to miss direct your attention they are doing all of these things in order to delay the coming of Biafra, to please their Janjaweed masters in the North.

“We have discovered their tricks, we know what they are up to, and we are determined to stop them, and that is what we are going to do every blessed day that we wake up, what we are going to do is to remind them always without fail that we remain as determined and as formidable as ever,” he said.
