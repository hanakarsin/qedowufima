Daisy Lowe - Age, Family, Bio

Fashion model who is most recognized as the child of Gavin Rossdale and Pearl Lowe.

Before Fame

She signed with Select modeling agency when she was 15 years old after a scout for the agency saw her walking around Camden Town.

Trivia

In 2011, she bared it all for her first Playboy Magazine spread.

Family Life

She dated actor Matt Smith in 2010 until their split in 2011. She briefly dated musician Thomas Cohen. Her father married Gwen Stefani and had three more children. In April 2023, she and her fiancé Jordan Saul welcomed a daughter named Ivy. They've been together since 2020.

Associated With

She once dated DJ and music producer Mark Ronson.
