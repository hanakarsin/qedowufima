These College Memes Are Here To Help You Get Over The Anxiety Of Finals

Finals are tough on your psyche but these hilarious memes are here to get your through your anxiety.

I procrastinate as much as the next human, but it's something I've been working on obliterating from my personality. If you think about the number of people's dreams and livelihoods that've been killed by laziness, I'm sure it'll rank up there as the number one cause of depression.

Nothing really feels better than going after what you need to go after and doing what you need to do despite your feelings, because those are the characters we admire most. Think about any book, novel, movie, or old-school story told by your grandparents. They were all filled with people who did what was right for the situation regardless of how they felt about it.

Which is why finals are such a stressful time. Usually in life you can get away with putting off stuff and no one is really going to hold you accountable, but when you're trying to graduate and not waste those tens of thousands of dollars in loans you signed your life away for, things can get pretty stressful. It's probably the biggest cause of seasonal depression for college students too cause it's just a lot to cram into a one week stretch. Combo that with the looming pain of having to spend break at home with your awkward family and you've got a recipe for disaster.

So don't let finals sneak up on you.

Which is why you need a healthy dose of memes to help you look at the lighter side of college.
