Jun 17, 2024 Lourie Helzer

Tabbes - Age, Family, Bio

Animator who publishes black-and-white animated short films to her Tabbes YouTube channel. She has garnered more than 2.3 million subscribers. Her animated shorts have often included stories from her life and rants about things that anger her. 

Before Fame

She registered for her YouTube channel in September of 2015, but did not publish her first video until April of 2016, which she called "Artist Problems."

Trivia

She publishes the drawings required for her animated shorts to her TabTabbes Instagram account. She often draws Pokemon characters as well. One of her most popular videos is about she didn't talk for three years in school when she was young. The video has been viewed over 19 million views since September of 2018. 

Family Life

She grew up with a younger brother in Nevada. Her extended family lives in South Korea.

Associated With

She featured Theodd1sout in her popular animation "Teachers."
