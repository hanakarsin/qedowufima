Mtangazaji wa Citizen awasisimua wengi katika ujumbe wake wa mapenzi kwa mkewe

Ni vigumu kujua yale wanawake hupenda kufanyiwa na wanawaume, lakini mtangazaji wa runinga ya Citizen ameamua kuwapa wanaume maneno matamu ya kuwaambia wapenzi wao

Habari Nyingine: Mbinu 6 za kushangaza ambazo wanawake wakenya hutumia ili kuwanyima wanaume NGONO

Mtangazaji wa runinga ya Citizen Mike Njenga ana kila sababu ya kujivunia kwa kumpa mkewe furaha tele kwa njia ya ujumbe.

Siku ya wapendanao (Valentine) imekaribia, na iko siku chache tu baada ya mkewe Mike, Phoebe Wanjiku kusherehekea siku yake ya kuzaliwa, Februari 8.

Habari Nyingine: Tazama kikundi kinachosakata ngoma UCHI mbele ya umati (picha 18+)

Katika huo mseto wa hisia, Njenga aliamua kumtumia mkewe ujumbe wa kumyeyusha moyo ambao kila mwanamke angependa kuusikia.

Alisema kuwa la maana maishani kama mwanaume, ni kukumbuka kuwa wewe ndiye mwenye nguvu. Na hiyo ina maana kuwa uwe tayari kumtetea mke wako na sio kumdhulumu.

Pata HAPA habari za TUKO.co.ke punde tu zinapochapishwa!

Mike alioana na Phoebe Wanjiku tarehe 11 mwezi Januari 2016.

Habari Nyingine: Wanawake ni MOTO! Kisa cha mke aliyetekwa na mipango ya kando

“A woman is an incubator- what you give her, she takes it incubates it and gives it to you double. You give her a sperm; she carries it for nine months and gives you as a baby. You give her a house; she decorates it and gives you a home. You give a grocery ; she processes it and gives you dinner. Whatever you give a woman, she takes it, incubates it and gives it to you twice, thrice, seven times better.”
