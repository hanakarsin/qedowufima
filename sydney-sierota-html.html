Sydney Sierota - Age, Family, Bio

Named one of MTV's 2014 Artists to Watch, she is the lead singer of the alt-pop band Echosmith along with her three brothers Jamie, Noah, and Graham. They released the single "Cool Kids" in 2013. 

Before Fame

She started playing in a band with her brothers when she was just 5 years old. 

Trivia

With Echosmith, she signed with Warner Bros Records in 2012 and released her first album in 2013.

Family Life

She began dating bassist Cameron Quiseng, whom she married in March 2019.

Associated With

She performed with Echosmith during the Teen Nick Top 10: New Year's Eve Countdown, hosted by Nick Cannon.
