Natalie Coughlin - Age, Family, Bio

American swimmer who won 22 gold medals from international competitions, and 12 total medals from Olympic events.

Before Fame

She broke the national high school record for the 100-yard backstroke.

Trivia

She was named NCAA Swimmer of the Year over three consecutive years.

Family Life

She married Crow Canyon Sharks swim coach Ethan Hall in April 2009. They have a daughter named Zennie together.

Associated With

She and Missy Franklin have both been honored as American Swimmer of the Year.
