Kenyan Lady Disgraced By Portable Breaks Silence

A Kenyan lady accused by street sensation, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable of approaching him for a hookup has shed more light on the viral video.

The lady identified as Peris Mumo disclosed that she reached out to the fast-rising singer and his friends for an interview for her YouTube channel.

The singer in a viral video had made jest of the lady alongside his friends as he said he was not available for hookup.

Mumo in her reaction to the development said she had to leave the scene when Portable and his friends started saying things she couldn’t understand.

Mumo further stated that she doesn’t understand why Portable accused her of approaching them for a hookup in the viral video, as her intention was quite different.

This is coming after Kenyan radio presenter, Maureen IImbi popularly known as Black Cinderella, threatened to sue Portable for his approach towards the lady.

According to Cinderella, the Nigerian singer was embarrassing her home country and insulting Kenyan girls.
