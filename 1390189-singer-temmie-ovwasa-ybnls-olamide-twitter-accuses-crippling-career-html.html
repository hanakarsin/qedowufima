Singer Temmie Ovwasa goes after YBNL's Olamide on Twitter, accuses him of crippling her career

- Singer Temmie Ovwasa has called out her former boss Olamide on social media

- The singer in a series of Twitter posts accused Olamide of not treating her well while she still in his YBNL record label

- Ovwasa alleged that the singer kept her for five years and she only put out four songs and videos within the time frame

Nigerian singer Temmie Ovwasa has left many surprised after taking to social media to call out her former boss and owner of YBNL Record, Olamide Adedeji.

Ovwasa took to her Twitter page with a series of accusations against the rapper noting that she will never remain silent about all she went through while in the label.

"My voice may not be loud but I fit shout. You don’t give a person hope & take it from them. Olamide Adedeji, I will always have your name in my mouth. Silence is not my language."
