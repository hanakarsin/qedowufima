Madi Webb - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star and pop singer who has gained popularity for the fashion, lifestyle, singing, and comedy videos she posts to her madibwebb account. Her fans have boosted her to over 2.5 million followers on the video platform. Her videos often revolve around being assigned a random outfit that she must create with her wardrobe. 

Before Fame

She launched her TikTok account in February 2020. 

Trivia

One of her most popular TikToks features her and Savannah Maddison lip-syncing to a comedic Mean Girls scene. Another popular video focuses on an outfit that she cannot get the right shoes for. It has earned over 2 million views. 

Family Life

Her father has appeared on her TikTok account. She has mentioned on her Instagram that her mom is a veteran.

Associated With

She posted a TikTok with rapper Tyla Yaweh in February 2020.
