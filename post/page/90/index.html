FG Bans Exportation Of Cooking Gas

Feb 14, 2024 The federal government of Nigeria has banned the exportation of cooking gas as part of efforts to beat down the price within the country and make the product available. This was made known on Thursday by the Minister of State, Petroleum Resources, Ekperikpe Ekpo. Speaking at a stakeholders workshop in Abuja with the theme, “Harnessing Nigeria’s Proven Gas Reserves for Economic Growth and Development,” Ekpo explained that once there is a stoppage of the export of locally produced domestic gas, there will be more volume for the domestic market which will automatically reduce the price of the product.
