Luke Mitchell - Age, Family, Bio

Australian actor who rose to prominence for portraying the character Lincoln on the ABC series Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. He also played the role of Roman on NBC's Blindspot and Captain John in CBS's The Code. 

Before Fame

He had aspirations of being a professional tennis player as a teen, but gave it up when he realized he wanted more from life. 

Trivia

He started out on the Australian soap opera Neighbours and is also known for playing the role of Will Benjamin in the series H2O: Just Add Water. 

Family Life

He proposed to actress Rebecca Breeds in 2012, and the couple married in January 2013.  

Associated With

His Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. costars include Chloe Bennet, Clark Gregg, and Ming-Na Wen.
