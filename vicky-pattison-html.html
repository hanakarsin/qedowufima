Vicky Pattison - Age, Family, Bio

Best known for appearing on the British reality show Geordie Shore. She went on to appear on the reality series Ex on the Beach and later her own show, Judge Geordie. 

Before Fame

She studied drama at Liverpool John Moores University. 

Trivia

Her success on Geordie Shore allowed her to become an MTV presenter on shows such as I'm a Celebrity: Extra Camp. 

Family Life

She dated fellow cast member Ricci Guarnaccio from 2011 to 2013. She has a sister named Laura. She began dating John Noble in 2016; they were briefly engaged a year later before calling things off. 

Associated With

Her co-star on Geordie Shore was Holly Hagan.
