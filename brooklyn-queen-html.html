May 29, 2024 Chauncey Koziol

Brooklyn Queen - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper who became a viral sensation with the release of her 2017 music video "Keke Taught Me." She is also the creative force behind songs like "Beat the Baby" and "Pretty Girl Stuff."

Before Fame

She grew up in Detroit, Michigan. She wrote her first song at age 5 and recorded her first song at age 8.

Trivia

Her video for "Keke Taught Me" was viewed more than half a million times in its first month on YouTube. She is a former member of GoatFamLA.

Family Life

She is the daughter of nail technician Nailtorious Kim. Her brother has a popular TikTok account at dyson121.

Associated With

The "Keke Taught Me" video features comedian Eastside Ivo.
