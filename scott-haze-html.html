Scott Haze - Age, Family, Bio

American actor known for portraying Lester Ballard in the 2013 crime drama film Child of God. More recently, he portrayed Frank Weaver in the 2021 supernatural horror film Antlers. His early work includes an appearance in a 2011 episode of CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.

Before Fame

He attended the Stella Adler Conservatory in New York City.

Trivia

He was included in Variety's 10 Actors to Watch for 2013. The list included such luminaries as Michael B. Jordan and Brie Larson.

Family Life

His mother is named Sherry Haze. He named his non-profit theater company, The Sherry Theater, after her.

Associated With

He is a close friend of actor James Franco. The two have collaborated on films together, including Child of God and As I Lay Dying.
