Marion Hall - Age, Family, Bio

Dancehall rapper formerly known as Lady Saw who had her third top 10 reggae album with "Walk Out" in 2007. She followed that up with "My Way" in 2010. She eventually retired from dancehall music and transitioned her focus to gospel music.

Before Fame

She made her first performances in Jamaica at age 15. She was formerly known as the Queen of Dancehall and Lady Saw.

Trivia

She won a 2002 Grammy Award for her No Doubt collaboration, Underneath It All. 

Family Life

She has a brother who performs under the name Sample.

Associated With

She has been compared to the female incarnation of Bob Marley.
