Jim Chapman (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube sensation known for popular vlogs such "A Guy's Advice for Girls" and "Draw My Life." He enjoys chatting about love, life, and manly things to his more than 2 million YouTube fans.

Before Fame

Some of his first vlogs were about rules for camping and Valentine's Day gifts for men.

Trivia

In June 2015, he posted a video with Connor Franta titled "YOUTUBER WHISPERS WITH CONNOR FRANTA!" He has been signed by Premier Models and Gleam Futures. 

Family Life

He was married to fellow YouTube star Tanya Burr from 2015 until 2019. He married Sarah Chapman at the Santa Barbara courthouse in March 2022. In September 2021 the couple welcomed their first child. His twin brother John and his sisters Samantha and Nicola are all YouTubers as well.

Associated With

He has collaborated with food expert Jamie Oliver on multiple videos.
