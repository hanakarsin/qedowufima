Certificate Or No Certificate, Tinubu Is Nigerias Next President MC Oluomo

The former Chairman of the National Union of Road Transport Workers in Lagos State, Musiliu Akinsanya, popularly known as MC Oluomo on Saturday said the presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress, Bola Tinubu would be the next president of Nigeria with or without a certificate.

MC Oluomo stated this while reacting to the controversy surrounding the certificate of the former Governor of Lagos State ahead of the 2023 general election.

Naija News had earlier reported that Tinubu told the Independent National Electoral Commission that his certificates were stolen by unknown people.

Tinubu stated this in an affidavit submitted to the electoral body as part of his eligibility filings for the 2023 presidential elections.

The APC presidential candidate in the documents released on Friday left the columns for his primary and secondary education unmarked.

The national leader of the ruling party, however, stated that he obtained a degree in business and administration in 1979 at the Chicago State University.

The development has seen a group call on the Inspector General of Police to arrest Tinubu.

MC Oluomo in his reaction to the developing issue in a post on his Twitter account wrote, “Certificate or No Certificate, Asiwaju is the next President of Nigeria. Who certificate help.”
