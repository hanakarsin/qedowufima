Caitlyn Taylor Love - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and singer who had her breakout role as Izzy Fuentes on the series I'm in the Band. From 2012 to 2017, she lent her voice to White Tiger on the Disney XD show Ultimate Spider-Man: Web Warriors.

Before Fame

She won a Miss Texas Pageant contest in 2004 as well as Miss Jr. Actress of the Year at IMTA New York in 2005.

Trivia

She also has a recurring voice acting role as Ava Ayala and White Tiger on the animated series The Ultimate Spider-Man.

Family Life

She was encouraged to pursue acting by her parents in Harlingen, Texas.

Associated With

She sang the song We Are The Kids for the 2010 TV movie Den Brother, which starred Hutch Dano .
