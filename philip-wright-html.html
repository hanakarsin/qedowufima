Philip Wright - Age, Family, Bio

Dancer and choreographer who has performed for artists such as Will Smith, Chayanne and Nicki Minaj. He has also worked as a dance instructor at the Millennium Dance Complex, Debbie Reynolds Dance Studio and the Abby Lee Dance Studio.

Before Fame

He was a hip-hop instructor at the Peaches School of Dance and Music in Miami, Florida.

Trivia

He has amassed over 430,000 Instagram followers by posting photos and videos of his students performing his elaborate routines.

Family Life

He is originally from Miami, Florida. He later moved to California to pursue his love of dance.

Associated With

He has worked at Abby Lee Miller's dance studio.
