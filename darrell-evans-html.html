Darrell Evans - Age, Family, Bio

MLB infielder who hit 414 home runs during his 21-year career.

Before Fame

He broke into the major leagues as a third baseman before becoming a designated hitter.

Trivia

He hit at least 100 home runs for three different teams during his career.

Family Life

He married and had three children with his wife LaDonna.

Associated With

He began his career with the Atlanta Braves, playing several years alongside Hall of Fame slugger Hank Aaron.
